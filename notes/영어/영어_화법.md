## 직접화법 vs 간접화법 — 기본 구조

**직접화법:** 말한 내용을 따옴표로 그대로 인용.
```
She said, "I am tired."
He asked, "Are you coming?"
```

**간접화법:** 말한 내용을 절로 전달. 따옴표 없음.
```
She said (that) she was tired.
He asked if I was coming.
```

**변화하는 것:**
1. 따옴표 → that절 / if절
2. 인칭대명사 → 전달자 기준으로 변경
3. 시제 → 한 단계 과거로 후퇴
4. 지시어·시간 표현 → 맥락에 맞게 변경

**변화하지 않는 것:**
- 현재도 사실인 내용(진리·불변 사실)은 시제 유지 가능
```
He said (that) the Earth is round.
```

## 시제 후퇴 (Backshift)

간접화법 전환 시 시제를 한 단계 과거로 당김.

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|---|---|
| am/is/are | was/were |
| will | would |
| can | could |
| may | might |
| have p.p. | had p.p. |
| was/were | had been |

```
"I am tired."      → She said she was tired.
"I will help."     → He said he would help.
"I have finished." → He said he had finished.
"I was sleeping."  → She said she had been sleeping.
```

**후퇴 안 해도 되는 경우:**
- 전달 동사가 현재형: `She says she is tired.`
- 현재도 사실인 내용: `He said water boils at 100°C.`
- 가정법: 이미 과거형이라 더 당길 필요 없음

## 인칭·지시어·시간 표현 변환

**인칭대명사 — 전달자 기준으로 변경:**
```
"I love you." (A가 B에게)
→ A said he loved her.
```

**지시어·장소:**

| 직접 | 간접 |
|---|---|
| this/these | that/those |
| here | there |
| come | go |

**시간 표현:**

| 직접 | 간접 |
|---|---|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| yesterday | the day before |
| tomorrow | the next day |
| last week | the week before |
| next year | the following year |

```
"I'll see you tomorrow."
→ She said she would see me the next day.

"I came here yesterday."
→ He said he had gone there the day before.
```

전달 시점이 같은 날이면 today/tomorrow 그대로 유지 가능.

## 의문문·명령문의 간접화법

**Yes/No 의문문 → if / whether:**
```
"Are you coming?"
→ He asked if I was coming.
→ He asked whether I was coming (or not).
```

**Wh- 의문문 → 의문사 + 평서문 어순:**
```
"Where do you live?"  → She asked where I lived.
"What time is it?"    → He asked what time it was.
```
❌ `She asked where did I live.` — 의문문 어순 그대로 쓰면 오류.

**명령문 → tell/ask + 목적어 + to부정사:**
```
"Close the door."  → She told me to close the door.
"Please sit down." → He asked me to sit down.
"Don't move."      → She told me not to move.
```

**전달 동사 선택:**
- 명령·지시: tell / 요청·부탁: ask
- 충고: advise / 경고: warn / 금지: forbid
