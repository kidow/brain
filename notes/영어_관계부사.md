## 관계부사란 — 관계대명사의 부사 버전

관계부사는 부사(구)를 대신해서 두 문장을 연결한다.

```
This is the city. + I was born in the city.
→ This is the city [where I was born].
                    ↑ "in the city" = where
```

| 관계부사 | 선행사 | 대신하는 것 |
|---|---|---|
| `where` | 장소 | in/at/on the place |
| `when` | 시간 | in/on/at the time |
| `why` | 이유 (the reason) | for the reason |
| `how` | 방법 (the way) | in the way |

---

## 관계부사 = 전치사 + which

관계부사는 `전치사 + which`로 바꿔 쓸 수 있다. 뜻 동일.

```
the city where I was born    = the city in which I was born
the day when we met          = the day on which we met
the reason why he left       = the reason for which he left
```

일상 → `where/when` 선호. 격식·문어체 → `in which / on which`.
독해 시 `in which` = `where`임을 즉시 인식하는 것이 핵심.

---

## when — 시간 선행사

선행사가 시간 표현(the day / the time / the year / the moment…)일 때 `when`.

```
I remember the day [when we first met].
That was the year [when everything changed].
```

선행사 생략 가능:
```
I remember when we first met.   (= the time when ~)
```

---

## why — 이유 선행사

선행사는 거의 항상 `the reason`.

```
That's the reason [why he left].
```

`the reason why` = `the reason that` = `the reason` — 셋 다 허용.
구어에서는 선행사만 남기고 why 생략이 가장 자연스럽다.

선행사 없이도 사용:
```
I know why he left.   (= the reason why ~)
```

---

## how — 선행사 없이만 쓴다

`the way`와 `how` 동시 사용 불가.

```
This is the way how he did it.  ❌
This is how he did it.          ✅ (how만)
This is the way he did it.      ✅ (the way만)
This is the way that he did it. ✅ (the way + that)
```

`how`는 항상 선행사 없이 명사절:
```
I don't know how she did it.
Show me how it works.
```

---

## 관계부사 선행사 생략

`where / when / why / how` 모두 선행사 없이 명사절로 쓸 수 있다.

```
Tell me where you live.       (= the place where ~, 목적어)
I remember when it happened.  (= the time when ~, 목적어)
I know why she left.          (= the reason why ~, 목적어)
Where she lives is a mystery. (주어)
The question is why he quit.  (보어)
```
