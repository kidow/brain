## 시간 전치사 in / on / at

핵심 논리: 범위가 클수록 in, 작을수록 at

| 전치사 | 범위 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **in** | 넓은 기간 (연·월·계절·아침/오후) | in 2024, in March, in summer, in the morning |
| **on** | 하루 단위 (요일·날짜) | on Monday, on March 5th, on Christmas Day |
| **at** | 특정 시점 (시각·순간) | at 3 o'clock, at noon, at midnight, at the moment |

함정:
- "아침에" → in the morning (아침이라는 긴 대역)
- "월요일 아침에" → on Monday morning (특정 날 우선, on 승)
- "정오에" → at noon (딱 찍힌 순간)

예문:
- I usually wake up in the morning.
- The meeting is on Friday.
- See you at 9.

## 장소 전치사 in / on / at

핵심 논리: 범위 크기 순 (시간과 동일)

| 전치사 | 범위 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **in** | 안에 둘러싸인 공간 | in the room, in Seoul, in Korea, in the box |
| **on** | 표면 위 접촉 | on the table, on the floor, on the wall, on the street |
| **at** | 특정 지점·장소 | at the bus stop, at the door, at the office, at school |

뉘앙스:
- at school(위치) vs in school(재학 중)
- 교통수단: 둘러싸인 것 → in a car / 탑승 플랫폼 → on a bus, on a train, on a plane

예문:
- I left my keys in the car.
- She's on the bus right now.
- Meet me at the entrance.

## 이동 전치사 to / toward / into / onto

핵심: 도달 여부 + 어디로

| 전치사 | 의미 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **to** | 목적지 도달 | go to Seoul, walk to the door |
| **toward** | 방향만 (도달 여부 모름) | walk toward the door |
| **into** | 안으로 진입 (동작) | walk into the room, jump into the pool |
| **onto** | 표면 위로 도달 (동작) | climb onto the roof, jump onto the table |

상태 vs 동작:
- in(상태) vs into(동작): She is in the room. / She walked into the room.
- on(상태) vs onto(동작): The cat is on the table. / The cat jumped onto the table.

onto vs over:
- onto = 올라가서 거기 착지 (목적지가 그 표면)
- over = 위를 넘어서 지나감

예문:
- He climbed onto the roof. (지붕에 올라가 있음)
- He jumped over the fence. (울타리를 넘어서 반대편으로)

## 투과·통과 전치사 through / across / along / past

핵심: 어떻게 통과하냐

| 전치사 | 의미 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **through** | 내부를 관통 (3D 공간 안을 지남) | through the tunnel, through the window, through the forest |
| **across** | 표면을 가로질러 (2D 평면 위를 지남) | across the street, across the bridge, across the room |
| **along** | 선을 따라 이동 | along the river, along the road, along the corridor |
| **past** | 옆을 지나침 (멈추지 않음) | walk past the shop, drive past the school |

through vs across:
- 터널 → through (안을 관통)
- 다리 → across (표면 위를 건넘)

through vs beyond:
- through = 관통해서 반대편 도달
- beyond = 넘어선 저편 (더 먼 곳)

예문:
- She looked through the window. (창문을 통해서)
- There's a city beyond the mountains. (산 너머 저편)
- He walked past the café. (카페 옆을 지나침)
- He walked through the café. (카페 안을 통과)

## 수직 위치 전치사 above / below / over / under

핵심: 접촉 여부

| 전치사 | 의미 | 접촉 여부 |
|--------|------|-----------|
| **over** | 바로 위 (덮거나 가로지를 수도 있음) | 접촉 가능 |
| **above** | 위 (단순 위치, 덮지 않음) | 접촉 없음 |
| **under** | 바로 아래 (덮여 있음) | 접촉 가능 |
| **below** | 아래 (단순 위치) | 접촉 없음 |

over vs above:
- over = 덮거나 이동 포함 가능
- above = 단순 높이·수치 비교

under vs below:
- under = 바로 아래, 덮여 있는 느낌
- below = 단순 낮은 위치·수치·층위

예문:
- She put a blanket over him. (담요로 덮음)
- The lamp hangs above the table. (떠 있음, 접촉 없음)
- The cat is under the table. (테이블 바로 아래)
- It's below zero today. (수치)

## 근접·분리 전치사 by / beside / between / among / near / from

핵심: 얼마나 가깝냐, 몇 개 사이냐

| 전치사 | 의미 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **near** | 가까이 (거리 막연) | near the station |
| **by / beside** | 바로 옆 (near보다 가까움) | sit by me, stand beside the door |
| **between** | 사이 (둘, 또는 명확히 구분된 것들) | between A and B |
| **among** | 사이 (셋 이상, 무리 속) | among friends, among the trees |
| **from** | 출발점·분리 | come from Seoul, far from home |

between vs among:
- between = 개수 2개 (또는 명확히 구분된 그룹)
- among = 3개 이상, 무리 속에 섞임

예문:
- The secret is between you and me. (딱 둘)
- She found her phone among the clothes. (옷더미 속)
- He's from Korea. / Far from perfect.

## 원인·수단·도구 전치사 by / with / because of / due to

핵심: 수단(어떻게) vs 도구(뭘 써서) vs 원인(왜)

| 전치사 | 의미 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **by** | 수단·행위자 | by car, by hand, painted by Picasso |
| **with** | 도구 (손에 쥔 것) | cut with a knife, write with a pen |
| **because of** | 원인 (구어) | late because of traffic |
| **due to** | 원인 (격식) | delayed due to bad weather |

by vs with:
- with = 도구 (뭘 손에 들고)
- by = 수단·방법 (어떤 방식으로) / 행위자 (수동태)

by 교통수단 → 관사 없음: by car / by bus / by train ✅ (by a car ❌)

예문:
- He opened the door with a key. (도구)
- He opened the door by pushing it. (방법)
- She was killed with a knife. / She was killed by a stranger.
- The game was cancelled due to rain. (격식)
- I was late because of you. (일상)

## 관련·주제 전치사 about / of / for / on

핵심: 얼마나 구체적·격식적이냐

| 전치사 | 의미 | 예시 |
|--------|------|------|
| **about** | ~에 관해 (일반·구어) | talk about money, a book about war |
| **on** | ~에 관해 (격식·학술) | a lecture on economics, an essay on climate |
| **of** | ~의 (소속·내용·구성·추상) | the smell of coffee, fear of failure |
| **for** | ~을 위해 / ~의 이유로 / 기간 | for you, famous for kimchi, for 2 hours |

made of vs made from:
- made of = 재료 그대로 (table made of wood)
- made from = 재료가 변형됨 (wine made from grapes)

for 기간 vs since 시점:
- for 2 hours (기간) / since 2020 (시작 시점)

예문:
- Let's talk about the problem. (일상)
- She gave a talk on quantum physics. (강연)
- This is for you. / I waited for 2 hours.

## 동사+전치사 고정 표현 (콜로케이션)

규칙 없음 — 통째로 암기.

on: depend on / focus on / insist on / congratulate sb on
with: agree with / deal with / help sb with / be satisfied with
for: apologize for / apply for / wait for / blame sb for
of: consist of / think of / be afraid of / remind sb of

함정:
- agree with (의견에 동의) — agree with his opinion ✅
- apologize for (행위에) / apologize to (사람에게) — 둘 다 씀
- wait for you ✅ (~~wait to you~~ ❌)

## 전치사 생략 규칙

장소 부사 — 전치사 없이 바로:
- go home ✅ (go to home ❌)
- come here / go there ✅
- 단, 소유격 붙으면 to 복활: go to my home ✅

시간 — yesterday/today/tomorrow/last~/next~/this~/every~ 앞엔 전치사 없음:
- yesterday / today / tomorrow ✅ (on yesterday ❌)
- last Monday / next week / this Friday / every day ✅

방향 내포 동사 — to 생략:
- enter the room ✅ (enter to the room ❌)
- reach Seoul ✅ / approach the table ✅

## 형용사 + 전치사 고정 표현

형용사 뒤 전치사는 논리보다 암기. 세트로 기억.

---

## 명사 + 전치사 고정 표현

명사 뒤 전치사도 고정. 논리보다 암기.

| 전치사 | 명사 | 예 |
|---|---|---|
| for | reason / demand / need / respect | reason for this change |
| of | advantage / lack / fear / example | lack of sleep |
| in | interest / increase / decrease / experience | increase in sales |
| to | solution / answer / key / exception | solution to the problem |
| with | problem / trouble / connection | problem with this approach |
| between | difference / relationship | difference between A and B |

자주 틀리는 것: reason **for** ✅ (of ❌) / answer **to** ✅ (for ❌) / key **to** ✅ (of ❌)

같은 명사도 전치사 따라 의미 다름:
- interest **in** (관심) vs interest **on** (이자)
- connection **with** (연관) vs connection **to** (연결)

---

| 전치사 | 형용사 | 예 |
|---|---|---|
| at | good / bad / skilled / terrible | She's good at math. |
| in | interested / experienced / involved | He's interested in design. |
| of | afraid / aware / capable / proud / tired | I'm proud of you. |
| with | happy / satisfied / familiar / bored / angry | Are you familiar with this tool? |
| for | ready / responsible / famous / sorry | She's famous for her paintings. |
| about | worried / excited / curious / passionate | I'm excited about the trip. |
