## 불규칙 비교급·최상급

| 원급 | 비교급 | 최상급 |
|------|--------|--------|
| good / well | better | best |
| bad / ill | worse | worst |
| many / much | more | most |
| little | less | least |
| far | farther / further | farthest / furthest |

- This coffee is better than that one.
- She has more experience than me.
- This is the worst movie I've ever seen.

farther = 물리적 거리 / further = 추상적 거리("더 나아가"). 일상 영어에선 further가 더 일반적.
