영어 수동태 치트시트

English Passive Voice — 구조·시제·패턴 한눈에

핵심 구조

능동 → 수동: 목적어 → 주어  |  동사 → be + p.p.  |  원래 주어 → by + 명사 (생략 가능)
The chef cooked the meal.  →  The meal was cooked by the chef.

시제별 수동태

시제구조예
현재is/are + p.p.The report is written.
과거was/were + p.p.The report was written.
미래will be + p.p.The report will be written.
현재완료has/have been + p.p.The report has been written.
과거완료had been + p.p.The report had been written.
현재진행is/are being + p.p.A bridge is being built.
과거진행was/were being + p.p.A bridge was being built.
조동사modal + be + p.p.It must be finished.

특수 패턴

get 수동 — 비격식
get + p.p.

He got fired. / She got hurt.
be = 격식·중립  |  get = 비격식·우발

사역 수동
have/get + 목적어 + p.p.

I had my car repaired.
She got her hair cut.
have = 중립  |  get = 비격식·노력

비인칭 수동
It is said that…  =  He is said to…

It is said / believed / reported / thought
과거: He is said to have been rich.

이중 목적어 (Ditransitive)
give / send / show / tell

They gave him a prize.
→ He was given a prize. 선호
→ A prize was given to him.

조동사 수동 — 과거 판단

구조뜻
must have been + p.p.~됐음이 틀림없다
should have been + p.p.~됐어야 했는데 (안 됨)
could have been + p.p.~될 수 있었는데 (안 됨)
might have been + p.p.~됐을지도 모른다

by vs with

by — 행위자 (누가)
The window was broken by the child.
with — 도구·수단 (무엇으로)
The window was broken with a rock.

고정 전치사 패턴

감정
be interested in~에 관심 있다
be surprised at/by~에 놀라다
be satisfied with~에 만족하다
be disappointed with/in~에 실망하다

관계·상태

상태 수동 vs 동작 수동

문장해석판별
The door is locked.문이 잠겨 있다상태
The door is locked every night.문이 매일 밤 잠긴다동작
by + 행위자 추가 가능 → 동작  |  get으로 교체 자연스러움 → 동작  |  둘 다 어색 → 상태

수동태 불가

자동사(목적어 없음) → 수동 불가   The accident was happened.
happen occur arrive appear sleep die
같은 단어도 타동사 용법이면 수동 가능   She runs the company. → The company is run by her.

수동태를 쓰는 이유

상황예
행위자 불명확·중요하지 않음My wallet was stolen.
행위자를 의도적으로 숨김Mistakes were made.
결과·대상에 초점Three people were injured.
