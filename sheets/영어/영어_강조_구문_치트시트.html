영어 강조 구문 치트시트

Emphatic Structures — do / cleft / 재귀대명사 / 어순 강조

do 강조

주어 + do/does/did + 동사원형 — 뒤 동사는 반드시 원형
예문뜻
I do like you.정말로 좋아해
She does know the answer.정말 알고 있어
He did call me last night.분명 전화했어

It-cleft (분열문)

It is/was + 강조 대상 + that(who) + 나머지
강조 대상예문
주어It was she that broke the window.
목적어It was the window that she broke.
부사구It was yesterday that he called me.

사람 강조 → who 가능. 부정: It was not she that broke it.

Wh-cleft (의사 분열문)

형태예문
What + 주어 + 동사 + is/was + 강조What I need is a break.
역전형A break is what I need.
All-cleft (오직 ~만)All I want is peace. / All she did was cry.

재귀대명사 강조

강조할 명사 바로 뒤 또는 문장 끝에 -self/-selves
구분예문생략 가능?
강조I did it myself.✅ 생략 가능
재귀I hurt myself.❌ 생략 불가

by oneself = 혼자서(alone), 강조 용법과 다름

부사 강조 — Gradable vs Ungradable

Gradable (very/extremely 등)Ungradable (absolutely/completely 등)
tiredexhausted
goodperfect
surprisedamazed
badterrible / awful
very tired / absolutely exhausted  |  very exhausted / very perfect

어순 강조 (전치)

목적어·보어를 문두로 — 주어·동사 어순은 유지 (격식·문어체)
예문
This book I have read three times.
Happy I was not.
This I can do. That I cannot. (대비 강조)
