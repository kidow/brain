영어 분사구문 치트시트

English Participial Constructions — 압축·유형·오류 한눈에

만드는 법 — 부사절 → 분사구문

접속사 제거
주절과 주어 같으면 주어 제거
동사 → -ing
Because I was tired, I went home. → Being tired, I went home.

분사 선택 — 능동 vs 수동

현재분사 -ing — 능동

주어가 직접 동작

Walking to school, I saw an accident. Hearing the news, she called me.
과거분사 p.p. — 수동

주어가 당하는 상태

Written in Korean, the book was hard. Excited by the news, she called me.
"~된 상태로" → p.p.  |  "~하면서·~하고" → -ing

의미 유형

의미예
이유Being late, he apologized.
시간Entering the room, she smiled.
조건Turning left, you'll see the bank.
동시 동작She sat reading a book.
연속 동작He stood up, walking to the window.
접속사 없으므로 정확한 의미는 문맥 판단. -ing 절 → 이유/시간/조건 중 자연스러운 것으로 읽기.

특수 형태

완료 — Having + p.p.

주절보다 먼저 일어난 일

Having finished, he went home. Having lived in Japan, he speaks Japanese. Having been told the news, she cried. (수동)
부정 — Not + -ing

not은 분사 앞에

Not knowing what to say, he was silent. Not having slept well, she felt tired. Knowing not ❌
독립 분사구문 — 주어 다를 때

주절과 주어 다르면 분사구문에 주어 명시

The weather being fine, we went out. It being Sunday, the shop was closed. All things considered, it was a success.
with 분사구문 — 부대 상황

with + 목적어 + -ing/p.p.   "~한 채로"

He sat with his arms folded. (p.p.) She walked in with her phone ringing. (-ing) with the door open (형용사)

Dangling Participle — 주어 불일치 오류

분사구문의 암묵적 주어 ≠ 주절 주어 → 논리 오류
Walking down the street, a dog bit me. ❌ (개가 걷고 있었다?) Walking down the street, I was bitten by a dog. ✅ Having studied hard, the exam was easy. ❌ (시험이 공부했다?) Having studied hard, I found the exam easy. ✅
확인: 분사구문을 완전한 절로 펼쳤을 때 주절 주어와 일치하는지 체크.
