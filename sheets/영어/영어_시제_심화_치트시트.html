과거 사건 + 현재 상태·결과 연결  |  yesterday / ago / last~ 와 함께 불가
용법키워드예

완료 계열 비교

현재완료 진행 — has been doing

과거부터 지금까지 계속·누적. for/since와 자주 씀.

I have been waiting for an hour. She has been studying since morning. I have been crying. (for 없어도 OK)

has done = 완료  |  has been doing = 진행 중

과거완료 — had done

"과거 속의 과거" — 어떤 과거 시점보다 더 이전.

When I arrived, she had already left. I realized I had forgotten my wallet. He had never seen snow before he moved.
과거완료 진행 — had been doing

과거 시점 이전부터 그 시점까지 계속 진행.

She had been crying when I found her. They had been dating for 3 years before they broke up.
미래완료 — will have done

미래 특정 시점까지 완료되어 있을 것. by + 시점.

By next year, I will have graduated. By the time you arrive, I will have finished cooking.

미래 표현 3가지

결정 시점특징예
will 지금 막 즉흥·예측·의지 "I'll get it." (전화 울릴 때)
be going to 이미 계획·근거 있는 예측 I'm going to visit Paris next month.
현재진행 이미 확정 약속·예약 I'm meeting her at 7.
Look at those clouds — it's going to rain. (근거 있는 예측 → be going to) "The phone is ringing." "I'll get it." (즉흥 → will)

상태동사 — 진행형 불가

상태동사는 진행형 불가   I am knowing the answer.
상태동사 (진행 불가)
know / believe / understand like / love / hate / want / need own / have(소유) / contain / seem
같은 동사, 뜻에 따라 달라짐
I am having a car. ❌ (소유) I am having lunch. ✅ (행위) I am seeing her tonight. ✅ (만나다)
