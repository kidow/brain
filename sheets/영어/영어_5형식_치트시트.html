영어 5형식 치트시트

모든 영어 문장은 5가지 패턴 중 하나 — 동사가 형식을 결정

5형식 전체 지도

1
S + V
The bird flew. / She arrived yesterday.
완전 자동사
목적어 불필요
2
S + V + C
She looks tired. (She = tired)
S = C
연결동사
3
S + V + O
I love coffee. / She broke the window.
완전 타동사
목적어 하나
4
S + V + O₁ + O₂
I gave her a gift. (her ≠ gift)
O₁≠O₂
간접+직접목적어
5
S + V + O + OC
We elected him president. (him = president)
O = OC
목적격 보어

2형식 — 연결동사(Linking Verb)

동사뜻예
be / become이다 / 되다He became a doctor.
look / seem / appear~처럼 보이다She looks tired.
feel / smell / taste / sound감각It smells good.
get / turn / grow / remain / stay상태 변화It got dark.
연결동사 뒤 = 형용사(보어). 부사 쓰면 틀림.
✗ She looks tiredly.   ✓ She looks tired.

4형식 → 3형식 전환

전치사동사예
to (전달)give, send, show, tell, teach, lendI gave a gift to her.
for (혜택)buy, make, cook, find, getShe made coffee for me.

4형식 vs 5형식 구별법

등호(=) 테스트: O = OC 성립하면 5형식
I gave her a gift. her ≠ gift 4형식
I made her happy. her = happy 5형식
We elected him president. him = president 5형식

5형식 목적격 보어 종류

보어 형태예동사 유형
명사They elected him president.임명·선출
형용사She made me happy.상태 변화
to부정사I want you to stay.want/tell/ask
원형부정사I saw him run.지각동사
현재분사I heard her singing.지각동사 (진행)
과거분사She had her hair cut.have (수동)

지각동사·사역동사 — 원형 vs to부정사

동사 유형보어예
지각동사 see/hear/feel/watch원형 or -ingI saw him run / running
사역 make (강제)원형She made me do it.
사역 let (허락)원형Let me try.
사역 have (지시)원형I had him fix it.
get (설득)to부정사I got him to fix it.
지각동사: 원형 = 행위 전체 목격 / -ing = 진행 중인 장면 목격

형식별 수동태

형식능동수동
3형식They built the bridge.The bridge was built by them.
4형식They gave her a gift.She was given a gift.
A gift was given to her.
5형식They elected him president.He was elected president.
지각/사역I saw him run.He was seen to run. (to 추가)

5형식 대표 동사

그룹동사
임명·선출elect, appoint, choose, name, call
상태 변화make, keep, leave, turn, drive
인식·판단think, find, consider, believe
지각see, hear, feel, watch
