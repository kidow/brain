감정동사 분사: -ing vs -ed

주어가 감정을 유발하면 -ing, 느끼면 -ed
동사-ing(유발)-ed(경험)
boreboringbored
interestinterestinginterested
exciteexcitingexcited
surprisesurprisingsurprised
confuseconfusingconfused
disappointdisappointingdisappointed
amazeamazingamazed

분사 위치 — 명사 앞 vs 뒤

형태위치예
단독앞a sleeping baby / a broken window
구(phrase)뒤a baby sleeping in the crib

분사의 보어 역할

형식예
주격 보어(2형식)She sat crying. / The door remained locked.
목적격 보어(5형식)I heard him singing. / She found the door locked.

목적어가 능동으로 하면 -ing, 수동으로 받으면 -ed

분사구문 만들기

① 주어 같으면 생략 ② 동사 → 분사 ③ 접속사 생략
Because he was tired→ Being tired (= Tired), he went to bed early.
While she was walking home→ Walking home, she met him.

분사구문의 의미 관계

의미예
시간/동시동작Walking home, she met him.
이유Being tired, she went to bed early.
조건Turning left, you'll see the bank.
양보Admitting his fault, he didn't apologize.

주절이 결과·상태 → 이유 / 주절이 사건 → 시간·동시동작

독립 분사구문

주절과 주어 다를 때 자체 주어 남김 — Weather permitting, we'll go on a picnic.
Generally speaking일반적으로 말하면
Strictly speaking엄밀히 말하면
Judging from ~~로 판단하면
Considering ~~을 고려하면

완료 분사구문

주절보다 먼저 일어난 동작: Having + p.p.
Having finished the report, she went home.
Having lost his wallet, he couldn't pay.
수동 완료: Having been + p.p. (= Warned before, having been 생략 가능)

단순 -ing = 동시/직전 / Having + p.p. = 확실히 먼저
